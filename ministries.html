Celebrate Recovery

Celebrate Recovery is a Christ-Centered recovery program based on the 12-Steps developed by Bill Wilson for Alcoholics Anonymous.
Applying the time-tested priciples underlying 12-step programs, Celebrate Recovery plants them in the rich soil of scripture, resulting in a harvest of new lives for those seeking freedom.
